Asgard Estate

An 18,000 sqm mixed-typology development in the heart of Mabushi - apartments, semi-detached and detached houses, terraces, a commercial block, and a playground, all within one gated community.

About this listing: Asgard Estate is developed by Vinland Architects & Designs, an Abuja-based architecture and real estate firm founded in 2020. Black Diamond Homes is proud to broker this development on Vinland's behalf. All renders and site imagery on this page are provided by Vinland and used with their permission.

Location & Layout

Set in the heart of Mabushi.

Asgard sits close to Minister's Hill, Banex Plaza, Emab Plaza, and the Nnamdi Azikiwe Expressway - placing residents within easy reach of Maitama and Wuse while staying tucked into a quiet, gated community.

  • 2 & 3-Bedroom Apartments
  • Semi-Detached & Detached Houses
  • Terrace Houses
  • Dedicated Commercial Block
  • Children's Playground

A community built for everyday life.

Tree-lined streets, a shared playground, and a private commercial block designed so residents rarely need to leave the estate for day-to-day errands.
