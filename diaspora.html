The Diaspora Problem

The questions every diaspora buyer asks.

Buying property from abroad comes with real risks - and real questions. Here's how we handle the ones we hear most.

Question

"How do I know the property is even real?"

Every listing includes verified title documentation and physical inspection reports. For completed properties, we arrange video walkthroughs so you can inspect before committing - no flying home required.

Question

"Who protects my money once I send it?"

Every transaction runs through our legal team, with documented payment milestones tied to construction or transfer stages. You're never wiring funds on trust alone.

Question

"What if there's a title dispute after I've paid?"

Title search and verification happens before any offer is accepted - not after. We confirm ownership history, encumbrances, and litigation status at the land registry upfront.

Question

"Who manages the property once I own it?"

For investors not relocating immediately, we can connect you with vetted property management partners for rental administration, maintenance, and tenant sourcing.
