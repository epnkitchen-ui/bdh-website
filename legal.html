Legal Services

Your investment, legally protected.

Every Black Diamond transaction includes comprehensive legal support - because in Nigerian real estate, what you don't know can cost you everything.

Why legal services matter.

Nigeria's real estate market offers extraordinary returns, but it also carries risks that many buyers - especially those purchasing from abroad - don't fully understand. Land disputes, forged documents, unapproved building plans, and contested titles are common. A single oversight can turn a dream investment into a legal nightmare.

At Black Diamond Homes, legal due diligence isn't an add-on. It's built into every transaction we handle. Whether you're buying directly or through our developments, our legal team ensures your investment is watertight from contract to keys.

Our Legal Services

Six safeguards, built into every deal.

01

Title Search & Verification

Before any transaction proceeds, we conduct a thorough title search at the relevant land registry to verify ownership history, confirm the property is free from encumbrances, litigation, or government acquisition, and ensure the seller has the legal right to transfer.

02

Survey Plan Confirmation

We verify that the survey plan matches the physical boundaries of the land, is duly registered with the Office of the Surveyor General, and does not overlap with adjacent plots or government-acquired land.

03

Contract Drafting & Review

Our legal team drafts or reviews all sale agreements, memoranda of understanding, and deeds of assignment to ensure your interests are fully protected. We negotiate terms on your behalf and flag any clauses that could expose you to risk.

04

C of O & Governor's Consent Processing

We handle the full application and processing of Certificates of Occupancy and Governor's Consent for property transfers within the FCT - document preparation, submission, tracking, and collection.

05

Property Due Diligence

For clients acquiring properties outside our developments, we offer standalone due diligence: physical inspection, document verification, regulatory compliance checks, and a written legal opinion on the transaction.

06

Dispute Resolution & Advisory

If you're facing a property dispute, boundary conflict, or title challenge, our legal team can advise on your options and represent your interests through mediation or formal proceedings.
